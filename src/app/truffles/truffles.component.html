Gourmet Truffles

At Ganache Chocolatier, truffles are really our specialty. It is what inspired the name of our shop! Each truffle has a center of ganache, a mixture of chocolate and cream, with a carefully decorated outer shell that indicates each flavor. We offer 13 different regular truffles, 6 liqueur truffles, 12 mini truffles, and 2 mini liqueur truffles. We even offer a small selectional of seasonal truffles.
